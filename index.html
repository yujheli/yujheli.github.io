About

I am currently a research scholar visiting at Carnegie Mellon University, and work with Prof. Kris Kitani. I am also working intensively with Prof. Yu-Chiang Frank Wang and co-advised by Prof. Yen-Yu Lin. My research interests involve Machine Learning (ML) and Computer Vision (CV), particularly representation learning and transfer learning.

During the past, I received my master degree in Vision and Learning Lab (VLL) at National Taiwan University (NTU) in 2019, where I was supervised by Prof. Yu-Chiang Frank Wang. I received my Bachelor of Science degree at National Tsing Hua University (NTHU) in 2017 and served as exchange student in my senior year at University of Minnesota, Twin Cities (UMN), where I worked with Prof. Chih-Lin Chi. I have also part-timely worked with Prof. Shou-De Lin in Fall 2017. To obtain more details about me, here is my Curriculum Vitae. (last updated in Sep. 2019)
