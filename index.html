About

I am now a graduate research student in Vision and Learning Lab (VLL) at National Taiwan University (NTU), supervised by Prof. Yu-Chiang Frank Wang. My research interests are in the areas of Machine Learning (ML) and Computer Vision (CV), particularly representation learning and transfer learning.

In past years, I graduated from National Tsing Hua University (NTHU) (in three years) and received my Bachelor of Science degree. Afterwards, I went to University of Minnesota, Twin Cities (UMN) as an exchange student, majoring in Computer Science. I also worked with Prof. Chih-Lin Chi for researching Bioinformatics and ML in UMN. After coming back to Taiwan, I worked with Prof. Shou-De Lin researching ML before seeking Master degree in VLL at NTU. To obtain more details about me, here is my Curriculum Vitae.
