Yu-Jhe (Jack）Li

My Chinese name is 李宇哲. I am currently a second-year (2020-) PhD student of Electrical and Computer Engineering (ECE) at Carnegie Mellon University (CMU), advised by Prof. Kris Kitani.

Prior to that, I served as a research associate at the Robotics Institute working with Kris Kitani for one year. In the past, I received my Master of Science degree in Vision and Learning Lab (VLL) at National Taiwan University (國立台灣大學) in 2019, where I was supervised by Prof. Yu-Chiang Frank Wang. I was also co-working with Prof. Yen-Yu Lin. I received my Bachelor of Science degree at National Tsing Hua University (國立清華大學) in 2017.
